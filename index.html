🦊 Meet Drip

🏝️ I used to play on a Minecraft server with my classmates. I had a base made of floating islands — a large one in the center, with smaller, less important ones surrounding it.

💧 The only way to reach them was a single stream of water that ran all the way down to the ground.
🚶‍♂️ As you can imagine, it was hard to bring mobs up there, and I felt kind of lonely.
🦊 I decided to bring up a companion — I found a fox holding an emerald and jokingly named him Drip.
📤 I brought him up the water stream and started building a dedicated island just for him.

Sadly, the bridges connecting the islands weren’t very safe, and Drip fell off to his death.

🌼 The island I had built for him became a graveyard, with only his tombstone on it.
💎 I placed his emerald on the grave, along with a book where everyone wrote a message to Drip.
🌟 Drip had been the mascot of the server for its entire life — losing him was a big moment.

👤 To honor him, I took on his name, so he would never be forgotten.
💬 I changed my Discord name to Drip, and it has stayed that way ever since.

🌈 Drip lives on, enjoying paradise.

Drip loves you and wants to be remembered.
